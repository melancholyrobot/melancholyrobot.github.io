About

Melancholy Robot is a four-piece band based in Glendale, CA. We call ourselves sadrock because we write sad rock.

Began as the solo project of singer and multi-instrumentalist Bijan, the original idea behind the name revolved around the concept of a robot programmed to feel only one emotion: SADNESS.

We have three full-length albums out right now: The Perfect Loser, The Book of Complaints, and the just-released Too Bored to Live, Too Scared to Die. We write, record, and mix everything ourselves in a bedroom.

Melancholy Robot is Bijan on vocals + guitar, Will on guitar + vocals, Elwin on bass, and Jordan on drums.
